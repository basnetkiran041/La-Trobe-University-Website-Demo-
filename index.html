Discover La Trobe!!

Welcome to La Trobe University, a exciting center of learning and Enterprise in Melbourne, Australia. Founded in 1964, we boast an excellent academic tradition coupled with comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate courses that suit varied interests and career goals. Our stunning campuses are designed to be collaborative and inspiring, set amidst beautiful landscapes and a lively student life. At La Trobe, we don't just give students knowledge; we build leaders who can make a difference in their community and beyond. Come and discover a world of possibilities where your journey transforms into an avenue leading to personal and professional development.

Explore Our Campus

From the first step inside La Trobe University, explore a whole new world that is filled with knowledge and creativity. Set amidst beautiful landscapes with ultramodern architecture, our campuses form dynamic spots where the ideas flower and friends grow. Visualize yourself on a casual walk through our lush gardens, the place where nature meets innovation, or immerse yourself in interactive learning spaces that evoke your curiosity. Meet renowned international faculty leaders in their chosen fields and shaping the future with innovative research. La Trobe is where fascinating campus events, project work, and cultural diversity all meet. It is a place that triggers your passion for exploration toward a bright future. Welcome aboard.

Gardens and Green Spaces

Step into the magical gardens and greenery at La Trobe University, where serenity meets inspiration. Extensive, nicely landscaped grounds give you a peaceful retreat from the busy academic life. Wander through vibrantly flowering displays and lush, shaded pathways that create an inviting atmosphere to sit and ponder in. Find secluded nooks, just perfect for studying or consideration, wrapped by the soothing sounds of nature. Every garden showcases a tapestry of many flora, living examples of biodiversity and sustainability. Take a leisurely walk, picnic with friends, or simply breathe in fresh air-the gardens serve as an outstandingly beautiful backdrop to learn and connect. At La Trobe, nature is more than an element; it is part of the university experience, keeping creativity buoyant and well-being fostered.

Art Galleries

Welcome to La Trobe University Art Gallery-a vibrant hub that engages in artistic exploration and cultural enrichment. Here, an impressive collection reveals excellence in both contemporary and traditional works, while giving voice to a rich tapestry of diverse voices and perspectives. Regular exhibitions of local, national, and international artists offer visitors the opportunity to engage with thought-provoking pieces that challenge the status quo. The gallery also hosts workshops, lectures, and community events, fostering a lively dialogue between artists, students, and the public. Allow yourself to be inspired by the ambient atmosphere where art and education come together, and discover how creativity enlightens the world that surrounds us. From the keenest art enthusiast to the most inquisitive newcomer, the La Trobe Art Gallery invites you to explore, appreciate, and connect with the transformative power of art.
